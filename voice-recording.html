Recording tips
A clean 60-second sample is all we need to clone your voice accurately.
Quiet environmentFind a room with minimal echo and background noise. Close doors and windows.
Phone or laptop mic is fineYou don't need special equipment. Hold your device 8–12 inches from your mouth.
Read naturallySpeak at a normal pace, as if you're reading to yourself. Relaxed and clear wins over slow and stiff.
You can re-recordDon't worry about perfection. Listen back and re-record as many times as you need.
